Our Story at SIEI Bites

SIEI Bites is a student-driven digital canteen solution designed to bring comfort, convenience, and efficiency to your food experience at Siddhartha Institute of Engineering & Technology. Born out of everyday struggles like long queues, delayed orders, and limited visibility into menu items, SIEI Bites was built to redefine the way you interact with your campus canteen.

With a responsive, mobile-first platform, SIEI Bites allows students, faculty, and staff to explore the canteen menu, place orders in advance, and avoid unnecessary waiting. Whether you're in a rush between classes or planning your lunch from your hostel, our platform ensures a smoother, smarter dining experience.
